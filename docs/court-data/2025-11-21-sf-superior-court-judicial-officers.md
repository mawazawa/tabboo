## DVRO Statistics & Case Outcomes (2024)

### Filing Data

- **1,016 DVRO requests** filed in 2024
- **295 granted** (~29% approval rate, 1 in 3)
- **90% self-represented** (9 out of 10 survivors)
- **50% case clearance rate** (vs 82% statewide)
- **2/3 of cases** involve ongoing hearings after initial grant

### Demographics

- Predominantly **low-income Black and Latino women**
- Self-represented parties may face cross-examination by their abuser

### Systemic Issues (from SF Standard Investigation, Aug 2025)

**Case Backlogs**:
- Cases drag on for months/years with repeated hearings
- Survivors remain "tethered" to abusers through court system

**Representation Gap**:
- 90% self-represented vs often-represented abusers
- Creates power imbalance in courtroom

**Clearance Rate**:
- SF: 50% (half the state average)
- California: 82%

## Practical Tips for SF Court

### Before Filing

1. **Check eligibility** - DVRO requires specific relationship types
2. **Gather evidence** - Photos, texts, medical records, witnesses
3. **Visit ACCESS Center** - Get forms reviewed before filing
4. **Plan childcare** - Expect several hours at courthouse
5. **Consider transit** - Parking is expensive and limited

### Filing Process

1. **Go to Room 402** (Family Law Clerk)
2. **File by 12:00 PM** for same-day TRO consideration
3. **Expect 3-5 hour wait** for TRO hearing
4. **Bring copies** - Original + 2 copies of everything
5. **ID required** - For security and filing

### At Your Hearing

1. **Arrive 30 min early** - Security screening
2. **Check department monitors** - Room assignments change
3. **Check in with clerk** - Announce your presence
4. **Prepare to wait** - Cases heard in order
5. **Be concise** - Judges have heavy caseloads

### DVRO-Specific Considerations

- **90% chance you're self-represented** - Use self-help resources
- **Abuser may be represented** - Prepare accordingly
- **Multiple hearings likely** - 2/3 of cases continue past initial grant
- **Keep documentation** - For each hearing
- **Service of process** - Must serve respondent
